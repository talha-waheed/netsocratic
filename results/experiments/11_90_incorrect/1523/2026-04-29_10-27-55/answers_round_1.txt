Q: What is the exact destination subnet (in CIDR notation, e.g. 10.0.0.0/24) that traffic from strasbourg should reach when connecting to the remote network? (Why: impacts reachability)
A: Traffic from Strasbourg should reach the destination subnet 100.0.4.0/24.

Q: What is the exact router hostname you are referring to when you mention "the remote network"? (Why: impacts reachability)
A: The specification does not provide a specific router hostname for "the remote network." It only outlines reachability for Luxembourg, Basel, and Strasbourg.

Q: How many paths should be used for load balancing when routing traffic from luxembourg and basel? (Why: impacts load balancing)
