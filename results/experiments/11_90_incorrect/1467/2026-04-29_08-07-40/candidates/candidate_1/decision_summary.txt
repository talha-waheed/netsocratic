Routers modified: Frankfurt, Rotterdam, Praha

Reasoning:
1. **Reachability**: 
   - All required reachability is inherently satisfied by OSPF's redistribution of connected subnets.
   
2. **Load Balancing**:
   - For the load balancing requirement from Praha to 100.0.9.0/24, we need to ensure that there are 2 equal-cost paths.
   - The paths are:
     - Path 1: Praha → Frankfurt → Rotterdam → Amsterdam (total cost = 1 + 1 + 1 = 3)
     - Path 2: Praha → Strasbourg → Rotterdam → Amsterdam (total cost = 1 + 1 + 1 = 3)
   - Both paths have the same total cost of 3, satisfying the requirement for 2 equal-cost paths.

3. **Waypointing**:
   - The requirement does not specify any mandatory waypoints, so we do not need to enforce any high costs on bypass paths.

4. **Cost Adjustments**:
   - Adjusted costs on Frankfurt and Rotterdam to ensure they do not participate in the load balancing paths.
   - Set costs on the relevant interfaces in Praha to ensure the paths are equal.

Limitations (if any):
None
