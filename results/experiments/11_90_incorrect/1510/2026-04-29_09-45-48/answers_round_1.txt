Q: What is the exact destination subnet (in CIDR notation, e.g. 10.0.0.0/24) that traffic from geneva should connect to? (Why: impacts reachability)
A: Traffic from Geneva should connect to the following destination subnets: 100.0.1.0/24 and 100.0.4.0/24.

Q: What is the exact subnet that "the remote network" refers to? (Why: impacts reachability)
A: "The remote network" refers to the subnets: 100.0.1.0/24 and 100.0.4.0/24 for Geneva, 100.0.4.0/24 for Luxembourg, 100.0.9.0/24 for Frankfurt, 100.0.1.0/24 for Bucharest, 100.0.4.0/24 and 100.0.1.0/24 for Madrid, 100.0.1.0/24 for Milan, and 100.0.29.0/24 for Strasbourg.

- Luxembourg: 100.0.4.0/24
- Frankfurt: 100.0.9.0/24
- Bucharest: 100.0.1.0/24
