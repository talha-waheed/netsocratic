Routers modified: Amsterdam, Athens, Basel, Bucharest, Budapest, Frankfurt, Geneva, Rotterdam, Zagreb

Reasoning:
1. For the reachability of `100.0.4.0/24` from Sofia, Rotterdam, and Bucharest, the bypass paths must be set to 1000 cost. Therefore, paths are adjusted to ensure they pass through Basel.
2. For the reachability of `100.0.29.0/24` from Istanbul, Rotterdam, and Marseille, paths bypassing London are set with higher costs to ensure they arrive through London.
3. Maintaining equal-cost paths involves ensuring that two specific routes for each connection also share that cost.
4. All relevant routers have been modified to align with new OSPF cost settings.
5. Cost values on other routers were not modified as they did not require changes per routing rules.
