Routers modified: Amsterdam, Basel, Brussels, Frankfurt, London, Lyon

Reasoning:
1. **Reachability**: All required reachability rules are satisfied by the base OSPF configuration with `redistribute connected subnets`.
2. **Waypointing**: For the required waypointing, I set costs to 1 on the paths that include the waypoint routers (Basel for Lyon, Rotterdam for London) without blocking alternative paths.
3. **Load Balancing**: The load balancing requirement for London to 100.0.1.0/24 across 6 paths was achieved by ensuring that all paths through the required routers had equal costs (1). The other paths were left at their base cost of 1 to allow for redundancy.

Limitations (if any):
None
