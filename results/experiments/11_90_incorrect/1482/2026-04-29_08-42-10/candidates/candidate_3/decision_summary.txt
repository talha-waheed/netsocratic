Routers modified: Basel, Sofia, Rotterdam

Reasoning:
1. Basel is required as a waypoint for traffic from Sofia and Rotterdam to 100.0.4.0/24 (Budapest's subnet).
2. To satisfy the waypoint condition, the cost to routes bypassing Basel (to Sofia for Basel and to Amsterdam for Rotterdam) is set to 1000.
3. For Rotterdam to load balance traffic across exactly 3 paths to 100.0.4.0/24, while ensuring all include Basel, the cost is set to 1 for paths through Frankfurt (another local subnet) and back to Amsterdam from Rotterdam.
4. Each router has one path through Basel (cost 1) and blocks bypass paths (cost 1000) to ensure compliance.
5. Sofia and Basel have their respective paths set to ensure they all lead to the destination subnet via the defined waypoint.

Limitations (if any):
None
