Reasoning:

   - The bypass route from Brussels to Frankfurt is set to 1000 to ensure traffic must go through Basel.

   - Similar to Brussels, Frankfurt to Basel is kept at cost 1 while the bypass cost (to Brussels) is set to 1000.

3. Kiev is required as a mandatory waypoint for Warsaw to reach 100.0.1.0/24:
   - The link from Kiev to Warsaw is set to cost 1000 to block bypassing paths.
  
4. For load balancing, both Brussels and Frankfurt are required to have exactly 2 equal-cost paths to reach 100.0.4.0/24, thus kept at 1. The bypass paths are set high at 1000.
