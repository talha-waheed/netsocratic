Reasoning:

2. For Praha, the paths to 100.0.1.0/24 are:
   - Praha → Kiev → ... (valid path)
   - Alternative paths bypassing Kiev need to be blocked.
3. Set the cost of bypass paths from Praha to 1000, while keeping the cost to Kiev at 1.
   - Thus, the cost from Praha to 100.0.1.0/24 will be through Kiev.
