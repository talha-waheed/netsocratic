3. **Load Balancing**:
   - Istanbul has multiple paths to London (100.0.29.0/24). The paths through Sofia and Bucharest are kept at cost 1 to allow for 3 equal-cost paths. The costs on the other paths are adjusted to ensure they are higher than the paths through Sofia and Bucharest.

4. **Final Configuration**:
   - Amsterdam's costs to bypass Rotterdam are set to 1000.
   - Sofia's cost to bypass Rotterdam is set to 1000.
   - Istanbul's paths are configured to allow for 3 equal-cost paths to London.
